Centre for Excellence in Cyber Security at Vellore Institute of Technology, Bhopal has been established to offer research based contributions on main security issues related to Cyber Security and Digital Forensic. The Indian National Cyber Security Policy 2013 aims at the establishment of a think tank for foreign policy making, internal security, defense, strategic importance of cyberspace and to secure India's interests in 21st century.
